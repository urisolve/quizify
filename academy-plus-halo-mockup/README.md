# HALOgen: Natural and Multimodal Interactions in Human-Artificial Self-Learning Enviorments

HALOgen, derived from two key concepts. The first one comes with "HALO" referencing the foundational HALO framework, (*Adicionar link para uma referência do professor?*) and the suffix "gen" from generative, reflecting the platform's use of Generative AI to create and adapt educational content. The second one, HALOgen serves as a metaphor inspired by "halogen" elements used in lighting. Just as halogen bulbs illuminate spaces, HALOgen aims to help the students to illuminate their self-learning assisted journey.

This app is a proof-of-concept platform designed to enhance self-learning enviorments through the integration of Generative AI, Nature Language Processing (NLP), and gamification. Its primary focus is on dynamically generating educational content in the field of electrical circuit analysis, to promote both students engagement and motivation.

---

## Overview

The HALOgen project explores the application of AI technologies, specially Generative AI and NLP, in conjunction with gamification techiques, to create a dynamic self-learning platform. The goal is to improve the learning experience by providing personalized educational content and adaptive feedback to students.

HALOgen targets the following key objectives:
- **Content adaptivity**: Automatically generating educational content that adapts to the student's progress and performance;
- **Enhanced engagement**: Using gamification concepts to motivate students and maintain high levels of interest and participation;
- **Multimodal interactions**: Enabling voice-to-text and text-to-voice transcriptions to help the student to quickly clarify a doubt with a wider quantity of possibilities.
